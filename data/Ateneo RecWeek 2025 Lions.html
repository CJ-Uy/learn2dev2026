LIONS COORDINATING BODY
The LIONS CB is the dedicated team behind-the-scenes of how the sector functions collectively. It works closely with COA-M and with the Office of Student Activities so that the rights and privileges of the sector are still being maintained.
‍
The priority is being a key ally for independent organizations to the student body and the admin offices around Ateneo, so that they may be elevated.
